Timebook

Timebook is a social network of historical figures built from Linked Open Data found on DBPedia, Europeana and WikiQuote. The main question we want to answer is: what if social media existed before? can it be used to explore, learn and understand art movements and collections?

Joint work with Luca Chiarandini. Code available at github. Winners of the Social Inclusion category and Developers Award at Europeana's Hack4Europe hackathon in Barcelona. We also thank Conxa Rodà for her support and encouragement to present our work at the Museum Next conference.

Covered here: * * blog post at Museu Picasso * Coverage at "El País" (in spanish) * Press release at Europa.eu * blog post about presentation of Timebook at European Digital Assembly Agenda * presentation at Museum Next * interview on Linked Open Data at the Citilab Europeana Workshop
