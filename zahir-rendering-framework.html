Zahir: Rendering Framework

To become an engineer in computer science I designed and developed a framework for computer graphics, focused on implementation of shading algorithms, in particular non photorealistic techniques. The framework is called Zahir and it was implemented in C++. My advisor was María Cecilia Rivara.
