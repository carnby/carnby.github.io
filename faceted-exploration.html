Faceted Exploration

In the summer of 2012 (in the northern hemisphere), we moved (my wife and me) from Barcelona to San Francisco to do an internship at Identified. Identified was a startup about gamification of job-seeking social networks, plus candidate sourcing for recruiters (it was acquired by Workday). I fully designed and implemented a search engine that used visualization widgets plus faceted search and filtering. My mentor through the internship was James Fan. Thanks for all your guidance, James!
