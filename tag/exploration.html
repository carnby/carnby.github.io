Sentiment Visualization Widgets – visualization, time-series, sentiment analysis, exploration, dbpedia

After working on search engines previously, and the experience of Timebook, we started to explore the design space of visualizations in the context of exploration of historical figures. In particular, we focused on how history has judged historical figures, through the usage of sentiment analysis over Wikipedia biographies.

Faceted Exploration – search, exploration, internship

In the summer of 2012 (in the northern hemisphere), we moved (my wife and me) from Barcelona to San Francisco to do an internship at Identified. Identified was a startup about gamification of job-seeking social networks, plus candidate sourcing for recruiters (it was acquired by Workday). I fully designed and implemented a search engine that used visualization widgets plus faceted search and filtering. My mentor through the internship was James Fan. Thanks for all your guidance, James!

Linspiration – visualization, time-series, fun, exploration

Linspiration is a visualization technique implemented in d3.js to explore and compare time-series in a fun way. This was one of the first explorations in my PhD into designing new visualizations. It all started in the brainstorming sessions with Alejandro Jaimes, Luca Chiarandini, Pancho Tolchinsky and Meekal Bajaj. I have learned so much from them!

Presented jointly with Alejandro Jaimes at the 2012 Intelligent User Interfaces conference in Lisbon, Portugal.
